"You CAN fly without fear. You WILL fly without fear. You are DETERMINED to fly without fear."
  1. I know this sounds like a long-shot, but I think this came from a late-night (i.e. early-morning) UK T.V. phone-in/chat show called 'The James Whale Radio Show'. The show had something of a cult following, and was airing at the same time as 'Cure for Sanity' was being recorded.I distinctly remember an episode about irrational phobias, in which some minor celebrity had agreed to undergo hypnosis in order to cure their fear of flying. The hypnotist repeated the above line *many* times, over and over again (very tedious viewing - maybe that's why it sticks out in my mind so well). (It didn't work, by the way)
    [ John Shepard - shep@cix.compulink.co.uk ]
  2. This sample was taken from a relaxation/hypnosis cassette which was given to Clint by a therapist, so that he could deal with his fear of flying! He mentioned this in an interview with the NME and said he found it completely useless, except that it was good for a laugh! It apparently then dawned on him, that it would be great as an obscure sample. That's the story...Morning Glory.
