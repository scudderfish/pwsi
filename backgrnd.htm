Pop WILL Eat Itself
Background Info:

SAMPLING: In music, the practice of capturing segments of existing recordings and including these captured samples in a new work.


NOTE: Peter Cigehn (Peter.Cigehn@um.erisoft.se) maintains a fairly thorough Top Sampling Groups and Sources list that is regularly updated. The Poppies consistently rank in the top 5 groups using samples on this list.
Jon Van Oast (jon@kzsu.stanford.edu)


This list is an (admittedly futile) attempt to catalog every sample used by Pop WILL Eat Itself, as well as to identify the source material from which each sample was taken. It provides a unique opporunity for fan interaction, and hopefully enhances our appreciation of what the band is doing.



Graham Poppie on the subject of obtaining permission to use samples from the original artists:
"Anyway, you could ask somebody and end up paying for something which even the writer at the end of the day may not [recognise because] you've altered it so much. You've put it in a different context, chopped it up, whatever. So I suppose if you were idealogically sound you would ask... But I guess we aren't idealogically sound."
- Source: The Story of Pop (Part 48:'It's a steal'), BBC Radio 1FM

#Big thanks and good vibes to Shep ( shep@compulink.co.uk) for the above quote!#
